Welcome to aeGis!

    How-To:

To enable features:

	- To enable a feature in aeGis, simply slide the toggle in the upper right hand corner for that fragment to the ON position.

To customize activation SMS:

  - Click on the fragments specific Activation SMS EditText field
Then input your desired text passphrase to be used to trigger a specific feature.

To proceed back to the app, hit the back button.
